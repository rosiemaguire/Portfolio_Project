Hi, I'm Rosie!

Good morrow! I studied a Bachelor of Education (Primary) at QUT. I began working casually in Child Care (OSHC) during the second half of my degree and stayed in the field for eight years. In November 2020, I pivoted my career trajectory and began working with child care software. Since working in the field of technology, I have learned and used SQL (Particularly Microsoft SQL), engaged in a Python Course via Udemy, began a Web Development course, also via Udemy and I am currently lucky enough to be studying with She Codes Australia in their 2023 Brisbane Plus cohort.
